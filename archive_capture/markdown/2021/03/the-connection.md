## The Connection

March 23, 2021 / [TMJ California](https://tmjcalifornia.com/author/pwsadmin/) / Comments Off on The Connection

Is often discounted by many doctors as they are often not aware of some very obscure neurology (jaw alignment sensors have massive representation in reticular formation (location of movement origination and sensory integration); [see this article](http://parkinsonstmj.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/tmj_and_reticular_formation007.pdf)).

In addition, craniomandibular dysfunction causes “substance P” levels to become elevated, a known modulator of movement (substance P lowers cell membrane potential, thus causing cells to fire too readily; [see central sensitization](/web/20240812132838/https://tmjcalifornia.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/central-sensitization-1.pdf)).
